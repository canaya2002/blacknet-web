Quick rules
-----------
  - Minimum clear space around the mark = 25% of its height.
  - Minimum mark size on screen: 24 px. In print: 12 mm.
  - Never re-tint, re-color, rotate, outline, or add effects to the mark.
  - Do not place over busy photography. Use a solid surface or a 40% black scrim.
  - The mark must always be in pure white (#FFFFFF) or pure black (#0A0A0A).
