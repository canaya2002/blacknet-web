02 · Usage rules Brand Guidelines

Clear space, sizing, and don'ts

The mark needs room to breathe. The numbers below are not preferences — they are the floor.

Minimum clear space

Maintain padding equal to 25% of the mark's height on every side. Never let any element cross into that area.

Minimum size

On screen: 24 px tall. In print: 12 mm tall. Below those sizes the mark loses its corner curvature.

Hard don'ts Required

Do not re-color

The mark is only pure white (#FAFAFA) or pure black (#0A0A0A). No brand colors, no gradients on the mark itself.

Do not rotate or skew

Always present the mark upright with no perspective transforms or angled placements.

Do not outline

The mark is already an outlined letterform. Adding a second outline doubles its visual weight.

Do not add effects

No drop shadows, glows, bevels, or motion blur in static contexts.

Do not crop

The complete mark must be visible. Do not crop into the mark to fill a container.

Do not redraw

Always use master files. Never re-trace, re-letter, or attempt to recreate the mark.

04 · Typography Brand Guidelines

Typography

One sans-serif for everything. One monospace for chips, captions, and code. Never substitute.

Primary · Geist Sans

Your entire digital presence. Operated with AI.

Weights in use: Regular (400) · Medium (500) · Semibold (600). Avoid Bold or Black weights in marketing surfaces — they read as old web aesthetic.

Mono · Geist Mono

ALL SYSTEMS OPERATIONAL · v1.0 · /api/inbox

Used for: chips, badges, response-time captions, file paths, eyebrows on headings, status indicators. Always with 0.18em letter-spacing minimum.

Hierarchy

H1 / 56pt

Hero, page hero. Semibold, -0.04em tracking, balance text.

H2 / 32pt

Section headings. Semibold, -0.02em tracking.

H3 / 18pt

Card titles, sub-section heads. Semibold.

Body / 14–16pt — Lead with the point. The platform unifies social, reviews, ads, and AI in one place.

Body copy. Regular. Generous line-height (1.5).

05 · Voice and naming Brand Guidelines

Voice

Direct. Calm. Specific. Blacknel is a tool used by people who manage real brands with real stakes — not a hype machine.

We sound like Yes

  • "Inbox unified across 10 channels. No more tabs."
  • "Replies suggested in the brand's voice, in 80 ms."
  • "Audit trail by default. Nothing happens without a record."
  • "Built for agencies and multi-location brands in LATAM and the US."

We don't sound like No

  • "Revolutionize your social game with our cutting-edge AI!"
  • "The #1 platform for marketers who hustle 🚀"
  • "Unlock the power of your data with seamless synergy."
  • "It's giving brand-magic energy."

Naming

Always "Blacknel"

Capital B, lowercase rest. Never "BlackNel", "BLACKNEL", or "Black Nel".

The product is "Blacknel"

Not "the Blacknel platform" or "Blacknel SaaS". Just "Blacknel".
