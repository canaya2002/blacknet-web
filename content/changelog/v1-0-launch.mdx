After six months of closed beta with 40 pilot accounts, Blacknel v1.0 is open to everyone.

## What ships in v1.0

- **Unified inbox** across Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp Business, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, Google Business, and email.
- **Social publishing** to Feed, Reels, Stories, Shorts, and Threads on 10 networks.
- **Reviews & reputation** for Google Business, Yelp, TripAdvisor, Trustpilot, Apple App Store, and Google Play.
- **AI copilots** for inbox replies and post drafts. Trained on your brand voice docs.
- **RBAC** with five default roles, granular per-brand permissions, and audit log.
- **Multi-tenant architecture** with Row-Level Security at the database level.

## What's next

- Ads management (Q2 2026) — Google + Meta first.
- Listening & competitors (Q3 2026).
- Public API (Q4 2026, currently NDA on Enterprise).
