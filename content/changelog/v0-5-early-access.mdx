The first version of Blacknel is in the hands of 40 pilot accounts.

## What v0.5 includes

- Unified inbox for Facebook Messenger, Instagram DM, WhatsApp Business, Twitter/X, and email.
- Social publishing for Facebook, Instagram (Feed + Stories), LinkedIn, Twitter/X, and TikTok.
- Reviews monitoring for Google Business and Yelp.
- Basic AI for post copy and inbox suggestions.
- RBAC with three default roles.

## Known limitations

- Reviews response automation is manual only — coming in v0.7.
- No approval workflows yet — coming in v0.9.
- Ads management is not in scope for early access.

Pilot accounts get 12 months free in exchange for weekly feedback calls.
